About & Vision

From Spreadsheets to Autonomous Agents

My career did not begin in a tech lab; it began in the trenches of global e-commerce. From Karachi, I led end-to-end planning for 100+ Home & Kitchen SKUs in volatile Amazon markets across the EU, UK, Australia, and Canada.

Education & Credentials

MBA in Supply Chain Management (Karachi University) and BSc in Management Sciences (NED University). SAP MM, Six Sigma White Belt, Toyota 5S, and logistics certifications.

Operational Reach

100+ SKUs managed across EU, UK, Australia and Canada. Multi-market replenishment, on-time delivery, and demand-driven inventory strategies.

Tools & Systems

SAP MM, Oracle ERP, Odoo, advanced Excel dashboards, automated MIS, and MicroSaaS workflow tooling for supply chain operations.

The Foundation

Navigating Global Scale

In international supply chain, there is no room for guesswork. Every delayed shipment, miscalculated safety stock, or unoptimized purchase order directly bleeds working capital.

By aggressively refining reorder points and aligning replenishment with seasonal demand signals, I reduced stockout risk by 24% and improved weekly forecast accuracy to 83%.

I built resilient systems using traditional ERPs like SAP MM and Oracle, alongside highly advanced, custom Excel dashboards.

The Pivot

Hitting the Limits of Traditional Systems

While earning my MBA in Supply Chain Management from Karachi University, after a BSc from NED University, I saw a structural ceiling in enterprise operations.

Researching AI and machine learning integration made it clear that traditional systems are fundamentally reactive. By the time a dashboard flags an inventory imbalance, the margin is already compromised.

The future of logistics does not belong to better spreadsheets; it belongs to autonomous, Agentic AI.

The Builder

Vibe Coding and MicroSaaS

This realization led to the creation of "The Lab." Instead of waiting for massive enterprise software updates, I embraced "vibe coding"—using advanced AI assistants to rapidly prototype, iterate, and deploy MicroSaaS tools.

I build utilities that e-commerce sellers and logistics planners actually need daily, from dynamic FBA margin estimators to Buy Box trackers and workflow automation scripts.

My focus is not on flashy gimmicks; it is on high-fidelity, accurate tools that maintain professional credibility and solve real-world e-commerce pain points.

The Horizon

Redefining the Academic Standard

My operational experience and development work are driving toward a singular long-term goal: pursuing a PhD focused on the intersection of Agentic AI and global logistics.

I am committed to researching frameworks where AI agents do not just alert us to disruption, but autonomously negotiate with vendors, adjust lead-time buffers, and execute purchase orders in real-time.

I am an operator, a developer, and an academic. I am building the autonomous e-commerce ecosystems of tomorrow, today.
